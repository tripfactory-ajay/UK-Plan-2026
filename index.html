Licensing, Legal & Compliance

ATOL, ABTA, PSV Operator Licence, UK entity setup, M&A and regulatory requirements.

ATOL Licence

Civil Aviation Authority · Target Q4 2026
01

Type: Standard ATOL (Tour Operator) for flight-inclusive packages. Or Flight-Plus ATOL if flights and accommodation booked separately.

02

Requirements: Companies House registration, audited accounts, minimum net assets, APC bond (~£2.50pp to Air Travel Trust Fund), qualified management.

03

Preferred route: acquire a company with an existing ATOL — ATOL transfer is significantly faster than a fresh CAA application.

04

Timeline: Consultant Apr -> Financials May–Jun -> Submit Jul 2026 -> CAA review 8–12 weeks -> Grant Oct/Nov 2026.

05

Annual renewal each April. ATOL certificate issued for every qualifying sale.

PSV Operator Licence

Traffic Commissioner · Required for own coaches
01

Required to operate coaches commercially in the UK. Apply via DVSA / Traffic Commissioner.

02

Key requirement: CPC-qualified Transport Manager employed or contracted. Legal responsibility for compliance.

03

Financial standing: £8,000 for first vehicle + £4,450 per additional. Adequate maintenance arrangements required.

04

Drivers: PCV licence + Driver CPC qualification + tachograph compliance.

05

Apply Q3 2026. Expect 3–4 month processing. Operate under RHP in the interim.

UK Entity, ABTA & Tax

Companies House · ABTA · TOMS VAT
01

ORN Credit System — UK Launch

B2B soft launch Q3 2026, full B2C Q4 2026.

Regulatory priority: Obtain FCA legal opinion in Q2 2026 on whether ORN Credit constitutes a regulated payment product (Electronic Money Regulations 2011 / Payment Services Regulations 2017). FCA authorisation or e-money institution partnership may be required before launch.

Product Framework

Earn, Burn & Rules
01

Earn rates: 1 ORN Credit = £0.10. 5% on UK tours, 3% on European packages.

02

Redemption: minimum 50 credits, max 25% of booking value. Expire 24 months from last activity.

03

Eligible products: UK coach tours, European packages, ancillaries (insurance, visa, upgrades).

04

B2B earn: agents earn bonus credits for volume. 10 bookings/month = +1% earn rate.

Tech Build

Platform Integration
01

UK credit wallet: GBP-denominated per user/agent in TripFactory platform. Statement, history, redemption flow.

02

Booking confirmation -> credit allocation. Redemption at checkout -> deduction + booking amendment.

03

VAT treatment: credits awarded = no VAT event. Redeemed = discount on VAT-inclusive price. Tax advisor sign-off required.

04

India tech brief Q2 2026. Staging environment ready Q3 for B2B pilot.

Launch Plan

B2B -> B2C -> Europe
01

Q3 2026 — B2B Pilot: 20 UK agent partners. Earn credits on UK tour bookings. Feedback loop.

02

Q4 2026 — B2C Launch: consumer wallet. Double credits on first booking. Target 500 users.

03

Q1 2027 — European expansion: France, Poland, Germany rollout roadmap. Standardise earn rates.
